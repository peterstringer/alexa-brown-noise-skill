Just Brown Noise – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Overview

Just Brown Noise is an Alexa skill that plays continuous brown noise. It does not require an account and is designed to work without collecting personal information.

Data we collect

The skill stores a single piece of technical data:

  • Playback offset – the position in the audio track when you pause, so playback can resume where you left off. This is stored in an Amazon S3 bucket managed by the Alexa-hosted skill infrastructure.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, voice recordings, or location.

Third-party services

The skill runs on Amazon's Alexa-hosted infrastructure (AWS Lambda and S3). Your interaction with the skill is subject to Amazon's Alexa Terms of Use and Amazon's Privacy Notice.

Children's privacy

The skill is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
